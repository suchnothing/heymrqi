Howdy, Gaymers!

This day-by-day guide will instruct you on my personal methods for reaching perfection quickly. This guide will not work for chicken-mains or Joja-shills. Sorry :(. As you check items off your checklist in each season, your total progress will be compiled below. Here are my recommended parameters to follow as you use this guide:

  • When creating your new save file, open settings and check the box that says "Guarantee Year 1 Completable".
  • Start with Forest Farm to give you a headstart on mushroom collection and hardwood.
  • Farming profession: Tiller and Artisan
  • Mining profession: Miner and Blacksmith
  • Foraging profession: Gatherer and Botanist
  • Fishing profession: Fisher and Angler
  • Combat profession: Fighter and Brute

    • Please note: this guide uses local-storage to save your checklist progress directly to your browser cache. It may not work if you have some kind of blocker, and your checklist progress may disappear if you clear your cache. It also won't transfer to other devices or browsers.
