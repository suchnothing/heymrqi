    • FALL GUIDE

    General Advice

    1. If Jodi asks you to bring a largemouth bass, wait until Year 2 and do it when Kent is back.
    2. The first rainy day, go fishing in the river for walleye. If you catch an iridium one, keep it for the stardew valley fair.
    3. As you collect forage items, make sure to keep 5 common mushrooms for the dehydrator. All 5 need to be the same quality.
    4. At least one broccoli needs to go in the shipping bin, but you can sell the rest or pickle it to give to Harvey. Be careful not to use up too many preserves jars, as you'll need to make caviar with your sturgeon roe as well.
    5. Keep an eye on your friendship levels this month. If you reach max hearts with someone, stop giving them gifts even if the guide says to. If you're keeping items specifically to gift to them, like flowers, you can sell the remaining ones.

    Fall 1, Monday

    1. Craft 2 more furnaces, and fill all 4 furnaces with copper.
    2. You’ll need 11 sprinklers to fit the seeds for this month, so if you don't have that many already, craft enough now and place them. Clear out any dead plants and weeds, and till/water all the spaces around the sprinkler. It shouldn't take long with your upgraded tools.
    3. Harvest wheat, and plant 8 more around the same sprinkler.
    4. Craft another mayo machine and grab one of the small chests left from earlier. Put it with the other machines, and place a chest next to it. Ducks provide eggs every other day, so you will be turning one into mayo on the day you collect it, and saving the other in the chest to do on the off-day.
    5. For today, put one duck egg in the mayo machine, and put the other one in the shipping bin.
    6. Put the milk from your cows into the cheese presses. Put the cheese from yesterday into your cooking chest.
    7. Collect pine tar from the tapped trees and put it in your crafting chest.
    8. Fill your furnaces with iron.
    9. Go to Pierre's and buy 24 pumpkin (or 15 if you got the 9 free pumpkin seeds from the museum), 1 eggplant, 2 bok choy, 19 yam, 1 cranberry, 16 wheat, 8 fairy seeds, and 3 amaranth.
    10. Go back to your farm and pull out 16 of the beet seeds and the two rare seeds you bought earlier.
    11. Plant the 8 fairy seeds on an outer sprinkler, keeping in mind that you will be putting bee houses next to them. Plant 16 beets and all the pumpkin seeds. Plant the bok choy, amaranth, and 3 of the yam on the same sprinkler. Save the other yam seeds. Plant the eggplant, cranberry, and rare seeds on the same sprinkler, along with 4 broccoli. If you haven't found 6 broccoli seeds yet, keep those spots open and plant them as you find them. Plant the 2 rare seeds on their own sprinkler. If you want, you can put more wheat or broccoli in the empty spots around that sprinkler, but you don't have to, and it won't be tracked in this guide.
    12. Put your highest quality fertilizer on the pumpkins. If you run out, craft some normal quality fertilizer using sap to fertilize the rest of the pumpkins. Also fertilize the fairy rose, the rare seeds, and the broccoli. Don't fertilize anything else.
    13. Fill your furnaces with copper.
    14. Craft 2 bee houses and place them as close to the fairy rose as you can. Don’t harvest the fairy rose until the end of the season so that your bees can make fancy expensive honey.
    15. Find a spot on your farm that you don't plan to use in the immediate future and absolutely fill it up with tree seeds. You are going to need a lot of wood. So much wood.
    16. Fill your furnaces with iron.
    17. If you have time, go to the mountain to collect stone and catch a midnight carp. The carp doesn't come out until 10PM, so you may need to gather stone, take a break to catch the carp, and then go back to gathering stone.

    Fall 2, Tuesday (Penny's Birthday)

    1. Fill furnaces with copper.
    2. Craft 2 kegs and make 2 pale ales.
    3. Put the duck mayo in the shipping bin.
    4. Put the milk from your cows into the cheese presses. Put the two cheese from yesterday into your cooking chest. You should now have three cheese for cooking.
    5. Fill furnaces with iron.
    6. Pack your bag with: Fishing rod, axe, hoe, food, pickaxe, 5 gold bars, and gifts for Evelyn, George, Pierre, Caroline, Abigail, Maru, Harvey, Lewis, Alex, Clint, Jas, Vincent, and Penny. If you have museum donations or geodes (don't forget to save 1 frozen geode), bring those as well.
    7. Give gifts to Evelyn, George, and Alex at their house.
    8. Give gifts to Pierre, Caroline, and Abigail at the general store.
    9. Give gifts to Maru and Harvey at the clinic.
    10. Give a gift to Lewis outside his house.
    11. Check the new community board for quests. Always accept a quest, but only complete it if you have time. Look at what you're doing for the week and try to accept a quest that most closely aligns with what is already planned.
    12. If you don't have at least 250 stone in storage, try to gather it before Clint's closes, as you'll need it tomorrow and don't want to get caught with only 100 stone and no pickaxe like I did.
    13. Go to Clint's and upgrade your pickaxe to gold. Open geodes if you brought them.
    14. Give a gift to Clint.
    15. Give museum donations if you have any.
    16. Give a gift to Penny, Jas, and Vincent.
    17. Catch Angler.
    18. Gather wood from around the valley for the rest of the day. You'll need 1050 wood and 500 stone for the barn and coop upgrades. If you already have enough, still collect more wood as you'll need it for crafting as well.
    19. Put Angler in the smoker before you go to bed.

    Fall 3, Wednesday

    1. Fill furnaces with copper.
    2. Craft another cheese press and grab one of the small chests left from earlier. Put it with the other presses, and place a chest next to it. Goats are milked every other day, so you will be turning one into cheese on the day you collect it, and saving the other in the chest to do on the off-day.
    3. Harvest apples and put them in your donations chest.
    4. Put one duck egg in a mayo machine and one in your donations chest.
    5. You should be getting large milk now. Put one in the shipping bin and the other in your donations chest. Put one cheese into your donations chest and sell the other one. Going forward, make all milk into cheese and sell it (don't forget to save 1 gold star quality for the fair).
    6. If your goats have matured today, milk them. Put one goat milk in the shipping bin and one in the cheese press.
    7. Fill your furnaces with iron.
    8. Pack your bag with: Fishing rod, hoe, 500 wood, 200 stone, and gifts for Robin, Sebastian, Demetrius, Linus, and Dwarf.
    9. Go to Robin's and build a deluxe coop.
    10. Give Robin a gift, then go down to Sebastian's room to give him a gift as well.
    11. Find Demertius and Linus outside and give them a gift.
    12. Give Dwarf a gift.
    13. Go fishing at the waterfalls in the Cindersap Forest for a goby. Once you've caught one, fish at the Cindersap river for tiger trout until 6 PM. If you don't have at least 550 wood in storage, then fish until you catch one tiger trout and then chop wood until 6PM.
    14. At 6PM, switch to the ocean to fish for albacore and other ocean fish. Pick up your crab pots on your way to the docks. You'll be moving them to a new location.
    15. Around 11:30PM stop fishing and go place your crab pots in the river close to Clint's and fill them with bait. Keep refilling them every time you go by until you have the snails and periwinkle you need. Once you have all the crab pot fish, you can put the crab pots away or move them back to the ocean to gather extra lobster for Elliot.
    16. Go home to bed. Keep two of your highest quality sea cucumber for Wizard.

    Fall 4, Thursday

    1. Collect the pale ale and replace it with two grapes. Put one of the pale ale in the shipping bin and bring the other with you.
    2. Fill furnaces with copper.
    3. Sell your duck mayo. Starting tomorrow, put one duck egg in a mayo machine every day and sell the mayo.
    4. Collect the goat cheese and put it in the shipping bin.
    5. Find a place on your farm to plant any mahogany seeds you have, and put tree fertilizer on them. You will eventually need 210 hardwood (not including item crafting) so keep chopping, replanting and fertilizing until you have that much.
    6. Fill your furnaces with iron.
    7. Pack your bag with: Sword, ax, hoe, food, 5 gold bars, and gifts for Evelyn, George, Pierre, Caroline, Abigail, Maru, Harvey, Lewis, Alex, Clint, Penny, and Dwarf.
    8. Give the pale ale to Pam.
    9. Give gifts to Evelyn, George, and Alex at their house.
    10. Give gifts to Pierre, Caroline, and Abigail at the general store.
    11. Give gifts to Maru and Harvey at the clinic.
    12. Give a gift to Lewis outside his house.
    13. Give a gift to Penny under the big tree by the graveyard.
    14. Pick up your pickaxe and upgrade your ax to gold. Make sure you have at least 550 wood in storage, as you'll need it tomorrow to upgrade the barn. If you don't, try to gather it before Clint's closes.
    15. Give a gift to Clint.
    16. Go to the mines and give Dwarf a gift.
    17. If you haven’t reached the bottom, attempt to now. If you have, collect any ore you're low on, gather stone for building, or slay monsters for the monster slayer goals.
    18. If you get a diamond, keep it to give to Jodi on her birthday.

    Fall 5, Friday (Elliot's Birthday)

    1. Fill your furnaces with copper.
    2. Harvest wheat and plant 8 more around the same sprinkler.
    3. Harvest bok choy. Don't replant it, and leave those spots empty for now. One bok choy goes in your cooking chest, the other goes in the shipping bin.
    4. Collect honey. Put one in your donations chest and the other in the shipping bin.
    5. Collect oak resin and put it in your crafting chest.
    6. Hopefully you've found a dinosaur egg by now. Put it in the incubator in the coop. Technically, you could have done this after the previous coop upgrade, but they're rare to find so I assumed you may not have gotten one yet.
    7. Check fruit cave.
    8. Fill your furnaces with iron.
    9. Pack your bag with: Fishing rod, hoe, 550 wood, 300 stone, and gifts for Wizard, Marnie, Jodi, Sam, Emily, Haley, and Linus.
    10. Check traveling cart.
    11. Give a gift to the Wizard at his tower.
    12. Give a gift to Marnie at her house.
    13. Buy 2 rabbits from Marnie.
    14. Give gifts to Jodi and Sam at their house.
    15. Give gifts to Emily and Haley at their house.
    16. Go back to your farm and pet your new rabbits.
    17. Go to Robin's and build the deluxe barn.
    18. Find Linus outside and give a gift to him.
    19. Fish at the ocean for sea cucumber until 6 PM. Keep one for cooking, and keep the rest to gift to Willy.
    20. At 6PM go back to your farm and drop off anything you don't need. Pick up gifts for Sebastian, Robin, Demetrius, Willy, Leah, Gus, Shane, Elliot, and Pam. Try to make sure you bring a loved gift for Elliot, as it's his birthday.
    21. Fill furnaces with copper.
    22. Go to the Stardrop Saloon to give the gifts.
    23. Go fishing at the ocean for the rest of the night. If Elliot wasn't at the Saloon, visit him in his cabin on the beach to give him a gift before you go fishing.
    24. Fill your furnaces with iron.

    Fall 6, Saturday

    1. Fill furnaces with copper.
    2. Harvest eggplant and put it in your donations chest.
    3. Collect pine tar from tappers and put it in your crafting chest.
    4. Harvest apples. Put one in your cooking chest and the rest in the shipping bin. You can sell all your apples from now on.
    5. Collect goat cheese and keep it to give as gifts. Going forward, keep all goat cheese until Robin and Leah are full hearts. Then you can sell all the goat cheese you make.
    6. Collect duck mayo and put it in the shipping bin.
    7. Fill your furnaces with iron.
    8. Pack your bag with: Sword, pickaxe, hoe, watering can, food, 5 gold bars, and a gift for Sandy.
    9. Pick up your ax and upgrade your watering can to gold.
    10. Go get the trash book.
    11. Go to the desert, give a gift to Sandy, and pick up forage.
    12. Open the Skull Cavern and start working your way down. You won't be able to get very far right now, so focus on gathering as much iridium as you can for tool upgrades. If you find a pristmatic shard, immediately take it to the triangle of pillars outside the Cavern and turn it into a Galaxy Sword. It'll make exploring the Cavern a lot easier.
    13. Leave the cavern at 11:30 and use any omni geodes you found to buy up to 3 desert totems from the trader so you can come back quickly next time without waiting for the bus.
    14. If you got cloth from the mummies, keep up to 6 of them and sell the rest.

    Fall 7, Sunday

    1. Fill your furnaces with copper.
    2. If you have found 5 common mushrooms, put them in the dehydrator. Put the dried fruit in the shipping bin.
    3. Harvest beets. Plant the final 8 beet seeds around one of the sprinklers, and plant yam seeds around the other one. Save 10 of the lowest quality beets for a quest and put the rest in your cooking chest.
    4. Fill your furnaces with iron.
    5. Pack your bag with: Fishing rod, hoe, and gifts for Wizard, Marnie, Jas, Jodi, Sam, Vincent, Emily, and Haley.
    6. Check traveling cart.
    7. Give a gift to the Wizard at his tower.
    8. Give gifts to Marnie and Jas at Marnie's house.
    9. Buy 2 sheep and 1 pig from Marnie. If you don't have enough money for the pig (they're expensive) come back as soon as you do and buy one.
    10. Give gifts to Jodi, Sam, and Vincent at their house.
    11. Give gifts to Emily and Haley at their house.
    12. Gather wood until 6PM.
    13. At 6PM go back to your farm and drop off anything you don't need. Pick up gifts for Gus, Shane, Elliot, and Pam.
    14. Fill your furnaces with copper.
    15. Go to the Stardrop Saloon to give the gifts.
    16. Go fishing at the ocean for the rest of the night.
    17. Fill your furnaces with iron.

    Fall 8, Monday

    1. Fill your furnaces with copper.
    2. Harvest cranberries and put them in your cooking chest.
    3. Harvest amaranth. Don't replant, and leave those spots empty for now.
    4. Fill your furnaces with iron.
    5. Pack your bags, you're going on an adventure… you need 1 battery and 1 solar essence from your crafting chest, 1 rainbow shell and 10 beets from your donations/quest chest. You'll also need the amaranth and cave carrot you kept for quests, your hoe, 5 copper bars, 10 clay, 100 stone, and a gift for Sandy.
    6. Bring Marnie an amaranth and cave carrot.
    7. Get a quest from the Community Board on your way to Clint's.
    8. Go to Clint's and upgrade your hoe to gold.
    9. Go to the bus tunnel and get Linus’ basket.
    10. Go into the bus tunnel and put the battery pack in the compartment.
    11. Go to Robin's and build the silo.
    12. Find Linus and return his basket.
    13. Put the rainbow shell in the box at the train station.
    14. Go to Lewis' house and put the beets in the fridge.
    15. Go to the desert and put the solar essence in the mouth of the big skeleton.
    16. Give Sandy a gift and pick up desert forage.
    17. Go home and check your lumber pile.
    18. Fill furnaces with copper.
    19. Chop wood and replant trees if you cut any wood on your farm.
    20. Fill your furnaces with iron before bed.

    Fall 9, Tuesday

    1. Fill your furnaces with copper.
    2. Harvest wheat, and plant 8 more around the same sprinkler.
    3. Collect honey and put both in your crafting chest. All honey can be sold going forward.
    4. Collect maple syrup and put one in your cooking chest and one in the shipping bin.
    5. Milk goats. If you get large goat milk today, save one for the community center and put one in the shipping bin. The goat milks are regular, make goat cheese to give as gifts.
    6. Fill your furnaces with iron.
    7. Pack your bag with: Fishing rod and gifts for Evelyn, George, Pierre, Caroline, Abigail, Maru, Harvey, Lewis, Alex, Clint, and Penny. If you have museum donations or geodes (don't forget to save 1 frozen geode), bring those as well.
    8. Give gifts to Evelyn, George, and Alex at their house.
    9. Give gifts to Pierre, Caroline, and Abigail at the general store.
    10. Give gifts to Maru and Harvey at the clinic.
    11. Give a gift to Lewis outside his house.
    12. Break open geodes if you brought them.
    13. Give a gift to Clint.
    14. Give a gift to Penny.
    15. Donate to the museum if you have anything.
    16. Fish at the ocean all day and save sea cucumbers to gift to Willy.

    Fall 10, Wednesday

    1. Fill furnaces with copper.
    2. Look after your animals and then fill your furnaces with iron.
    3. Pack your bag with: Sword, pickaxe, food, and a gift for Sandy.
    4. Use a desert totem to warp to the desert. Give a gift to Sandy and pick up forage.
    5. Go to the skull cavern.
    6. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore before bed.

    Fall 11, Thursday (Jodi's Birthday)

    1. Collect your wine from the kegs. Keep one of them for the community center and put one in the shipping bin. Put one wheat in one of the kegs, and a honey in the other keg.
    2. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore. If you don't have enough, don't worry. You'll get lots more in the future. Just smelt iridium whenever you can.
    3. Harvest corn and keep 5 gold star corn and 1 normal quality for the community center. Put one corn in the shipping bin. Keep two lowest quality for cooking. Save the rest to make oil.
    4. Harvest eggplant and put it in your cooking chest.
    5. Harvest yams. Till and water all the empty spots around that sprinkler and plant yams in all of them.
    6. Collect pine tar and use it to craft 2 looms. Put one loom in the coop and one in the barn.
    7. Shear your sheep. Put one wool in a loom and the other in the shipping bin. If you get wool from your rabbits, put that in the looms too. If you ever have more wool than looms, save it in one of the chests and put it in a loom the next day.
    8. Pack your bag with: Fishing rod, 5 gold bars, and gifts for Jodi, Evelyn, George, Pierre, Caroline, Abigail, Maru, Harvey, Lewis, Alex, Clint, and Penny. If you have museum donations or geodes (don't forget to save 1 frozen geode), bring those as well.
    9. Give a gift to Jodi at her house.
    10. Give gifts to Evelyn, George, and Alex at their house.
    11. Give gifts to Pierre, Caroline, and Abigail at the general store.
    12. Buy the following seeds from Pierre: 24 pumpkin, 24 wheat, 24 yam. Save them for replanting throughout the rest of the season.
    13. Give gifts to Maru and Harvey at the clinic.
    14. Give a gift to Lewis outside his house.
    15. Give a gift to Clint. Pick up your hoe and upgrade your trash can to gold.
    16. Give a gift to Penny.
    17. Go fishing at the ocean for the rest of the day.
    18. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.

    Fall 12, Friday

    1. Collect mead and put it in the shipping bin. Brew green tea leaves in the empty keg.
    2. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.
    3. Collect cloth and save it for crafting/building or sell it if you already have enough.
    4. Collect oak resin and put it in your crafting chest.
    5. Check fruit cave.
    6. Pack your bag with: Fishing rod, axe, hoe, and gifts for Wizard, Marnie, Jas, Vincent, Emily, and Haley.
    7. Check traveling cart.
    8. Give a gift to the Wizard at his tower.
    9. Give gifts to Marnie and Jas at Marnie's house.
    10. Give gifts to Jodi and Vincent at their house.
    11. Give gifts to Emily and Haley at their house.
    12. Gather wood until 6PM.
    13. At 6PM go back to your farm and drop off anything you don't need. Pick up gifts for Sam, Sebastian, Willy, Demetrius, Robin, Leah, Gus, Shane, Elliot, and Pam.
    14. Go to the Stardrop Saloon to give the gifts.
    15. Fish at the ocean for the rest of the night.
    16. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.

    Fall 13, Saturday (Abigail's Birthday)

    1. Collect beer and tea and put them in the shipping bin. Do not refill the kegs.
    2. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.
    3. Harvest cranberries and put them in your cooking chest. You'll need 4 in total. Try to keep 4 lowest quality, and then sell the rest you harvest.
    4. Harvest wheat, and plant 8 more around the same sprinkler.
    5. Harvest beets. You'll need 7 beets for cooking, so keep your 7 lowest quality in your cooking chest. Put one of the next lowest quality in the shipping bin. Keep the rest to gift to Evelyn in case you run out of tulips. If you get to full hearts with Evelyn, you can sell the rest of the beets.
    6. Plant yams where the beets were.
    7. Pack your bag with: Sword, pickaxe, hoe, food, desert totem, and a gift for Abigail.
    8. Pick up the gold trash can from Clint.
    9. Give a gift to Abigail. She will most likely be at Pierre's.
    10. Use the desert totem to to the desert and pick up forage.
    11. Go to skull cavern and collect as much iridium ore as possible. Don't forget to buy more desert totems before you go home if you're running low.
    12. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.

    Fall 14, Sunday

    1. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.
    2. Harvest and replant pumpkins. Put 3 lowest quality away for cooking, one highest quality away for a quest, keep one highest quality for the Stardew Valley Fair, keep one lowest quality for the community center, and sell the rest of the mid-high quality. Keep the rest of your lowest quality to put in kegs.
    3. Craft 2 more kegs.
    4. Put 4 of your lowest quality pumpkins in the kegs. Every time the pumpkin juice is done, replace it with more pumpkins and put the juice in the shipping bin. If you don't have any low quality pumpkins because you got lucky and harvested a bunch of mid-high quality ones, then put 42 of the mid-quality pumpkins in the kegs and sell the rest. After that, you can brew any remaining hops you have until the hops are gone.
    5. Shear your sheep and put the wool in the looms.
    6. Pack your bag with: Fishing rod, hoe, and gifts for Wizard, Marnie, Jas, Jodi, Vincent, Emily, and Haley.
    7. Check traveling cart.
    8. Give a gift to the Wizard at his tower.
    9. Give gifts to Marnie and Jas at Marnie's house.
    10. Give gifts to Jodi and Vincent at their house.
    11. Give gifts to Emily and Haley at their house.
    12. Go fishing for any fish you're missing. If you have all the fish you need, either fish at the ocean to get sea cucumbers for Willy or, if you already have some in storage, go fishing at Cindersap forest for tiger trout to sell.
    13. At 6PM go back to your farm and drop off anything you don't need.
    14. Pack your bag with: Gifts for Gus, Shane, Elliot, and Pam, and the following Community Center donations: common mushroom, wild plum, hazelnut, blackberry, corn, eggplant, pumpkin, yam, 5 gold star corn, 5 gold star melons, large milk, large brown egg, large white egg, large goat milk, duck egg, cloth, goat cheese, cheese, honey, jelly, 1 tree fruit (any), sunfish, catfish, shad, tiger trout, walleye, bream, eel, 10 wheat, 10 hay, and 3 apples. You will need to empty some tools and food out of your bag to fit it all.
    15. Donate community center items.
    16. Go to the Stardrop Saloon to give the gifts.
    17. From the community center donation prizes, sell the fall seeds, place the bee house, preserves jar, cheese press and keg next to the other same types of items, and place the heater in your coop. You can use, sell, or trash the glow ring.
    18. Chop wood for the rest of the night. If you don't have an iridium quality walleye for the Fair, fish at the Cindersap River after 10PM and try to get a gold or iridium quality midnight carp instead.
    19. Fill your furnaces with gold ore.

    Fall 15, Monday (Sandy's Birthday)

    1. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.
    2. If you don’t have two extra quality sprinklers, craft them now to keep the greenhouse crops watered. Also craft a seedmaker to put in or near the greenhouse.
    3. Inside the greenhouse, plant ancient seeds and fruit tree saplings if you have them. You’ll want one of each sapling and 8 ancient fruit plants in the greenhouse.
    4. If you got the seeds/items for the Year 2 crops from the travelling cart, plant them in the greenhouse around your second sprinkler and put your highest quality Speed-Gro fertilizer on them. You will need 5 red cabbage, 3 artichoke, 4 garlic, and one extra garlic seed to plant on Ginger Island. If you don't have enough, put the products you grew or bought into the seedmaker to make new seeds. Hopefully you'll get multiple seeds from one product. Keep making seeds and regrowing until you have enough of each item. If you're still missing any seeds, continue checking the travelling cart and plant any you get as soon as you find them.
    5. Craft as many kegs as you can and place them in the greenhouse, taking care not to put them too close to your fruit saplings. Brew your normal quality pumpkins in the greenhouse kegs if you have them. If you have any pumpkins left over, save them to put in the kegs when this batch is done brewing. If you have no pumpkins to brew, you can brew any extra hops you have from the summer and sell the IPA.
    6. Harvest your corn and save it for making oil.
    7. Collect cloth and save it for crafting. If you already have enough cloth saved you can sell any cloth you make.
    8. Go buy any fruit tree saplings you're missing from Pierre. Don't forget you already have an apple tree growing outside that you'll move into the greenhouse at the end of the season so you don't need to buy another apple.
    9. Check the community board for a quest.
    10. Go back to your farm to drop off everything, plant your fruit tree saplings in the greenhouse, and pack your bag with: Sword, pickaxe, hoe, food, and a gift for Sandy.
    11. Go to the desert, give Sandy a gift, and pick up forage.
    12. Go to the skull cavern.
    13. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.

    Fall 16, Tuesday (Stardew Valley Fair)

    1. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.
    2. Harvest eggplant and put it in your cooking chest.
    3. Milk goats and make goat cheese to give as gifts.
    4. Collect pine tar and put it in your crafting chest. You can now sell all future pine tar when you collect it.
    5. Gather wood and stone until 12pm.
    6. Collect your items for the display and attend the stardew valley fair. If you were unable to get all the items on the list, substitute for the next closest thing you can. Make sure you don't have 2 of the exact same item, and don't have more than 2 of the same type of item (ie. fruit, vegetable, gem, artisan, etc). Talk to everyone, put your items in the display, then talk to Mayor Lewis to be judged.
    7. Once you have your winnings, play the fishing or slingshot game a bunch to get the rest of the tokens you need so you can buy the stardrop and rarecrow. It’s not a bad idea to get enough tokens to also buy the prize ticket. You can also get anything else you feel like.
    8. Pick up your grange display items. Anything you don’t pick up can be retrieved from the lost and found box in the Mayor’s house later. If you included a diamond in the grange display, don't sell it. Save it to give to Marnie on her birthday.
    9. After the fair, collect wood and stone until bedtime.
    10. Fill your furnaces with gold ore.

    Fall 17, Wednesday

    1. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.
    2. Harvest wheat, and plant 8 more around the same sprinkler.
    3. Harvest and replant yams.
    4. Shear your sheep and put the wool in the looms.
    5. Hopefully your new baby... lizard?... hatched today. Make sure to pet it every day like the rest of your animals. Don't incubate any more dinos yet, even if you're lucky enough to have extra eggs, because you'll need the incubator for something else in a few days.
    6. Pack your bag with: Sword, pickaxe, hoe, food, and a gift for Sandy.
    7. Use a desert totem to warp to the desert, give Sandy a gift, and pick up forage.
    8. Go to the Skull Cavern and collect as much iridium ore as possible.
    9. Fill your furnaces with gold ore.

    Fall 18, Thursday (Marnie's Birthday)

    1. Collect pumpkin juice from the kegs and put it in the shipping bin. Move two of your original 4 kegs into the greenhouse, and keep 2 separate for any random brewing needs that may come up.
    2. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.
    3. Harvest cranberries and put them in the shipping bin.
    4. Collect maple syrup and keep one for a quest and the other for the Community Center. You can sell all other maple syrup going forward.
    5. Your pig should start finding truffles soon so craft 2 oil makers and put them somewhere convenient. Putting them near the cheese presses would be convenient as you can use the chest to store extra truffles if you have more truffles than oil machines.
    6. Collect cloth and save it for building/crafting. If you already have enough cloth, sell it.
    7. Pack your bag with: hoe, and gifts for Marnie, Evelyn, George, Pierre, Caroline, Abigail, Maru, Harvey, Lewis, Alex, Clint, and Penny. If you have museum donations or geodes (don't forget to save 1 frozen geode), bring those as well.
    8. Give a gift to Marnie at her house. If she isn't there, she may be at the clinic.
    9. Give gifts to Evelyn, George, and Alex at their house.
    10. Give gifts to Pierre, Caroline, and Abigail at the general store.
    11. Give gifts to Maru and Harvey at the clinic.
    12. Give a gift to Lewis outside his house.
    13. Give a gift to Clint.
    14. Give a gift to Penny.
    15. Go back to your farm to drop off stuff you don't need, and pack your bag with whatever you need to complete the community board quest for this week. If it's already completed, or if you can't work on it today for some reason, then pack what you need for the mine. Also pack gifts for Robin, Sebastian, Demetrius, Linus, and Dwarf, and give the gifts to them at some point before 5PM (when Robin's closes).
    16. Fill your furnaces with gold ore.

    Fall 19, Friday

    1. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.
    2. Harvest corn. You should have more than enough corn now to keep 2 for cooking and 11 for making oil. Move your corn for oil to the chest closest to your oil makers, and make them into oil whenever you pass by until they're all finished.
    3. Pack your bag with: Fishing rod, ax, hoe, a pumpkin for Caroline, and gifts for Wizard, Marnie, Jas, Jodi, Vincent, Emily, and Haley.
    4. Check traveling cart.
    5. Give a gift to the Wizard at his tower.
    6. Give gifts to Marnie and Jas at Marnie's house.
    7. Give gifts to Jodi and Vincent at their house.
    8. Give gifts to Emily and Haley at their house.
    9. Give a pumpkin to Caroline.
    10. Gather wood until 6PM.
    11. At 6PM go back to your farm and drop off anything you don't need. Pick up gifts for Sam, Sebastian, Willy, Leah, Gus, Shane, Elliot, and Pam.
    12. Go to the Stardrop Saloon to give the gifts.
    13. Go fishing at the ocean for the rest of the night.
    14. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.

    Fall 20, Saturday

    1. The next time the bookseller is in town, buy Way of the Wind parts 1 and 2.
    2. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.
    3. Pack your bag with: Sword, pickaxe, hoe, and food.
    4. Use a desert totem to warp to the desert and pick up forage.
    5. Go to the Skull Cavern and collect as much iridium ore as possible.

    Fall 21, Sunday (Robin's Birthday)

    1. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.
    2. Harvest wheat, and plant 8 more around the same sprinkler.
    3. Harvest eggplant and put it in the shipping bin.
    4. Harvest yams. Don't replant. Keep the yams to give as gifts, and sell any remaining parsnip.
    5. Decide where you want to place your windmill and make sure the spot is clear.
    6. Craft a trap bobber and attach it to your fishing rod.
    7. Pack your bag with: Fishing rod, hoe, 4 cloth, 50 stone, 150 wood, and gifts for Wizard, Marnie, Jas, Jodi, Vincent, Emily, Haley, and Robin.
    8. Check traveling cart. Buy the snowman rarecrow if it's 4000g, as it'll be 5000g from the Festival of Ice so you may as well get a discount now.
    9. Give a gift to the Wizard at his tower.
    10. Give gifts to Marnie and Jas at Marnie's house.
    11. Give gifts to Jodi and Vincent at their house.
    12. Give gifts to Emily and Haley at their house.
    13. Give Robin a gift and buy the mill.
    14. Bring your fishing rod with a trap bobber and a gift for Krobus. Save your gold quality pumpkin for his birthday though, and give him something else.
    15. By now, you should have gotten the sewer key from Gunther. Open it and go buy the stardrop and a void egg from Krobus (if you haven’t gotten one from the witch yet).
    16. Fish for mutant carp in the sewer.
    17. At 6PM go back to your farm and drop off anything you don't need. Put the void egg in the incubator and the mutant carp in the fish smoker. Pick up gifts for Gus, Shane, Elliot, and Pam.
    18. Go to the Stardrop Saloon to give the gifts.
    19. If you haven't caught the mutant carp yet, go back to fishing. If you have, chop wood until bed time.
    20. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore and sell the smoked mutant carp.

    Fall 22, Monday

    1. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.
    2. Craft as many kegs as you can and place them in the greenhouse.
    3. Collect your pumpkin juice from the greenhouse kegs and brew any remaining normal quality pumpkins. Put the juice in the shipping bin and mark juice off your checklist.
    4. Pack your bag with: Sword, pickaxe, hoe, desert totem, and a gift for Sandy.
    5. Go to the community board and pick up the weekly quest. Don't pick anything that will require your pickaxe this week, as you'll hopefully be upgrading it.
    6. Warp to the desert, give a gift to Sandy, and go to the skull cavern.
    7. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.

    Fall 23, Tuesday

    1. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.
    2. Harvest cranberries and sell.
    3. Harvest yams and keep them to give as gifts. Do not replant.
    4. Harvest corn and keep some for oil if you need it, or sell it all.
    5. If you got a dinosaur egg today, put it in a mayonaise machine.
    6. Put 21 wheat, 6 beets, and all the rice you kept into the mill.
    7. Pack your bag with: hoe, pickaxe, 5 iridium bars, and gifts for Evelyn, George, Pierre, Caroline, Abigail, Maru, Harvey, Lewis, Alex, Clint, and Penny. If you have museum donations or geodes (don't forget to save 1 frozen geode), bring those as well.
    8. Give gifts to Evelyn, George, and Alex at their house.
    9. Give gifts to Pierre, Caroline, and Abigail at the general store.
    10. Give gifts to Maru and Harvey at the clinic.
    11. Give a gift to Lewis outside his house.
    12. Upgrade your pickaxe to iridium and break open geodes.
    13. Give a gift to Clint.
    14. Give a gift to Penny.
    15. Work on completing your weekly quest.
    16. If you finish your quest early or can't work on it, go fishing at the river until 6PM and then switch to the ocean. If you haven't caught all the cave fish, you can work on that instead if you want.
    17. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.

    15. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.

    Fall 27, Saturday (Spirit's Eve)

    1. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.
    2. Craft as many kegs as you can to put in the greenhouse with the others.
    3. Harvest pumpkins, corn, and yams. Do not replant. Make sure you have at least one lowest-quality pumpkin for crafting, and one gold quality pumpkin for Krobus' birthday. Fill the greenhouse kegs with pumpkins, starting with the lowest quality first, and sell any remaining. Sell the corn. Keep the yams to give as gifts.
    4. Your odd-looking chicken should have hatched today so don't forget to pet it.
    5. Fish at the cindersap river until 7 PM.
    6. Chop wood until Spirit’s Eve starts.
    7. Go to Spirits Eve. Buy the jack-o-lantern recipe and the rarecrow. Solve the maze.
    8. When you get home, craft a torch, then use it and one of your lowest quality pumpkins to craft a jack-o-lantern. Make sure not to leave it out on the last day of fall, or it will rot.
    9. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.

    Fall 28, Sunday

    1. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.
    2. Harvest your fairy rose today. Put one in the shipping bin and save the rest to give to Jas if she’s not full hearts yet. If she is full hearts, sell them all.
    3. Harvest cranberries and sell.
    4. Craft a tea sapling. If you forgot to save wild seeds from earlier months, you can use the fall seeds you're about to craft right now. Put the sapling in the clay pot Evelyn gave you, or just sell it. You shouldn't need any further green tea leaves to reach perfection if you stole enough from Caroline.
    5. Craft the Fall wild seeds and sell them.
    6. Use a scythe to cut grass on your farm until your silos are full. You'll know they're full when you stop getting hay while cutting. As soon as you notice you aren't getting hay, stop cutting and save the rest of the grass for later.
    7. Put the following in your shipping bin: 1 gold ore, 1 gold bar.
    8. Pack your bag with: Sword, pickaxe, hoe, and gifts for Wizard, Marnie, Jas, Jodi, Vincent, Emily, Haley, and Sandy.
    9. Check traveling cart. I would normally advise that you only buy what you need for the perfection run, but this time you can also buy a Tree of the Winter Star if you want, as a little treat for coming so far.
    10. Give a gift to the Wizard at his tower.
    11. Give gifts to Marnie and Jas at Marnie's house.
    12. If your barn doesn't have a heater yet, buy a heater from Marnie.
    13. Give gifts to Jodi and Vincent at their house.
    14. Give gifts to Emily and Haley at their house.
    15. Go to the desert, give Sandy a gift, buy 92 starfruit seeds, and go to the skull cavern for the rest of the day.
    16. Fill your furnaces with iridium ore.
