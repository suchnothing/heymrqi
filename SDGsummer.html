    • SUMMER GUIDE

    General Advice

    1. You may get green rain one day this month. When it happens, abandon all other plans and harvest moss. Chop down 3 or 4 giant ferns, but don't waste time chopping down trees. Whatever plans got abandoned, pick up necessary ones (like big purchases or upgrades) the next day.
    2. On the first day of normal rain, go to the beach before 7pm to catch 1 red snapper. Fit the rest of your day around it however it makes sense.
    3. After donating all the dwarf scrolls, the next time you go to the mines open her shop and buy new recipes and books from her.
    4. As you collect forage items, make sure to keep 5 grapes and 5 of another forage fruit for the dehydrator. All 5 need to be the same quality.

    Summer 1, Monday

    1. Put some iron in the smelters.
    2. By now you will hopefully have the ability to make quality sprinklers. Make up to 10 sprinklers and place them in a convenient spot. If you can’t make them all, plant your crops as if there will be sprinklers then just continue to make more as you get the materials until all your crops are being watered.
    3. Till and water enough spaces for all the sprinklers.
    4. Put some iron in the smelters.
    5. Go to Pierre’s and buy 8 melon (you should have 9 at home from the community center), 3 tomato, 4 blueberries, 6 pepper, 17 wheat, 4 radish, 16 poppies, 8 spangle, 1 hops, and 8 sunflower. Also get 1 apple sapling.
    6. Save one of the melon seeds and one of the wheat seeds for a quest later. Plant 8 wheat, 2 radish, 8 poppies, and the rest of the seeds you bought. Save the extra wheat, radish, and poppy seeds for when they're ready to harvest and replant.
    7. If you have coffee or squash seeds, make another sprinkler to plant them next to.
    8. Put quality fertilizer on the melon seeds. If you have extra quality fertilizer, put it on your starfruit seeds.
    9. If you have lots of sap, put regular fertilizer on the flower, hot pepper, and blueberry seeds.
    10. Put some iron in the smelters.
    11. Find somewhere convenient but relatively out of the way to plant your apple sapling.
    12. Gather wood for the rest of the day. At this point you should have made all your sprinklers and still have at least 25 copper bars and at least 2 iron bars in storage. If you don't, then keep going back to the smelters while chopping to refill them until you have what you need.
    13. If you can, make a worm bin. Upgrade it to a deluxe after the green rain day. If you can't make a worm bin right now, make one as soon as you have the materials. Prioritize making the sprinklers first.
    14. If you need smelted materials put it in the smelters before you go to bed.

    Summer 2, Tuesday

    1. Put some iron in the smelters.
    2. Pack your bag with: Fishing rod, hoe, pickaxe, 5 copper bars, and gifts for Evelyn, George, Pierre, Caroline, Abigail, Maru, Harvey, Demetrius, Lewis, Alex, Clint, and Penny. If you have room to bring museum donations or geodes (don't forget to save 1 frozen geode), bring those as well.
      1. Bring a normal quality parsnip for anyone you don't have a loved gift for (double check that they don't hate it first).
    3. Before you drop off your pickaxe, make sure you have at least 200 stone in storage. If you don't, gather the stone first.
    4. Give gifts to Evelyn and George at their house. If you get there early enough (before they open) you may be able to catch Alex leaving the house and skip going to the beach to get him.
    5. Give gifts to Pierre, Caroline, and Abigail at the general store.
    6. Give gifts to Maru and Harvey at the clinic.
    7. Give a gift to Lewis outside his house.
    8. Give a gift to Alex at the beach.
    9. Go to Clint's and upgrade your pickaxe. Open geodes if you brought them.
    10. Give a gift to Clint.
    11. Make museum donations if you have any.
    12. Give a gift to Penny.
    13. Give a gift to Demetrius by the fountain.
    14. Fish at the Cindersap river until 6PM, then switch to fishing off the docks. If you're running low on space, leave the river at 5:30PM and drop stuff off at your farm. Save one rainbow trout to make bait later. Save one fish roe for tomorrow.
    15. Put some iron in the smelters before you go to bed.

    Summer 3, Wednesday

    1. Put some iron in the smelters.
    2. Gather and sell strawberry jelly. Add a parsnip and a fish roe to the preserves jars.
    3. Gather wood until 9:00am (or longer if you don’t have 300).
    4. Put some iron in the smelters.
    5. Pack your bag with: Fishing rod, hoe, 300 wood, 100 stone, and gifts for Robin, Linus, and Sebastian. Sebastian doesn't like parsnip so you should consider bringing him a frozen tear if you've collected a bunch. Don't worry too much about taking one out of your building chest for him, as you'll likely find many more before you need them.
    6. Go to Robin’s and build a coop.
    7. Give a gift to Robin.
    8. Give a gift to Linus by the lake.
    9. Fish at the lake. Smoke 1 sturgeon for the Luau and keep 3 uncooked.
    10. Give a gift to Sebastian when he comes to the lake to smoke, then fish at the ocean for the rest of the night.
    11. Put some iron in the smelters before you go to bed.

    Summer 4, Thursday (Jas' Birthday)

    1. Put some iron in the smelters.
    2. Make a trap bobber and attach it to your fishing rod.
    3. Pack your bag with: 5 iron bars, fishing rod, hoe, deluxe bait if you have it, and gifts for Leah, Willy, Jas, Alex, Clint, Penny, Caroline, Evelyn, George, Pierre, Maru, Harvey, Demetrius, and Lewis.
    4. Go straight to the dock by the tidepools and fish for octopus and Crimsonfish until 1PM.
    5. Give a gift to Leah at the tidepools if she's there.
    6. Give a gift to Willy at his shop. Sell any extra fish to him to clear out your inventory.
    7. Give a gift to Jas. She should be on the beach or walking to the beach from Marnie's ranch. If it's raining, she will be inside at Marnie's ranch.
    8. Give a gift to Alex at the ice cream stand.
    9. Go to Clint’s and upgrade your pickaxe to iron.
    10. Give a gift to Clint.
    11. If Caroline is in the town square, give her a gift.
    12. Give gifts to Maru and Harvey at the clinic.
    13. Give gifts to Evelyn and George at their house. If Evelyn isn't there, you'll find her tending the flowers somewhere in town.
    14. Give a gift to Pierre at the general store. Check if Caroline is there if you didn't catch her at the town square.
    15. If you have enough money, buy the backpack from Pierre.
    16. Give a gift to Demetrius by the fountain.
    17. Give a gift to Penny at the bench next to the Community Center.
    18. Give a gift to Lewis in the town square or the Saloon.
    19. Go back to the beach to fish off the docks for the rest of the day.
    20. Save 2 of your highest quality super cucumber to gift to Wizard this weekend. Smoke and sell the Crimsonfish.
    21. Put some iron in the smelters before you go to bed.

    Summer 5, Friday

    1. Put some quartz in the smelters.
    2. Harvest wheat and replant with more wheat. Keep 10 hay for the community center and keep the rest for your livestock. Sell gold and silver quality wheat. Put the normal quality in your donations chest until you have 10, and store the rest in your cooking chest until you've reached 23. After that you can sell them.
    3. Check fruit cave. Put a low quality fruit (like a salmonberry or blackberry) in a preserves jar to make jelly.
    4. Put some quartz in the smelters.
    5. Pack your bag with: Fishing rod, hoe, and gifts for Wizard, Marnie, Jas, Jodi, Vincent, Emily, and Haley.
    6. Check traveling cart.
    7. Give a gift to the Wizard at his tower.
    8. Give gifts to Marnie and Jas at Marnie's house.
    9. Give gifts to Jodi and Vincent at their house.
    10. Give gifts to Emily and Haley at their house.
    11. If it's raining, fish at the lake. If it's sunny, fish at the ocean. Stop fishing at 6PM.
    12. Go back to your farm and drop off anything you don't need. Pick up gifts for Sam, Sebastian, Willy, Leah, Gus, Shane, Elliot, and Pam. If you have a good amount of money, consider buying a salad for Leah and a beer for Shane at the bar.
    13. Put some quartz in the smelters.
    14. Go to the Stardrop Saloon to give the gifts.
    15. Fish at the ocean for the rest of the night.
    16. Put some quartz in the smelters.

    1. Put some quartz in the smelters.
    2. Gather and sell your pickles and aged roe, and mark them off your checklist.
    3. Harvest peppers. Put 1 in the shipping bin. Keep 1 for the Community Center and 3 for cooking. Keep all others to gift to Shane and Lewis.
    4. Chop wood until 8:30am.
    5. Go to Marnie and buy 1 white chicken and 1 brown chicken. Go back to pet the chickens, let them out of the coop to eat grass, and put hay in their feeder for rainy days.
    6. Put some copper in the smelters.
    7. Craft 10 lightening rods and place them somewhere on your farm.
    8. Pack your bags with: sword, hoe, 100 stone, 10 clay, 5 copper bars, food for the mines, and gifts for Sam, Robin, and Linus.
    9. Give a gift to Sam outside his house.
    10. Pick up pickaxe from Clint.
    11. Go to Robin’s and build a silo.
    12. Give a gift to Robin.
    13. Give a gift to Linus.
    14. Go as far as you can in the mines.
    15. Put some copper in the smelters before you go to bed.

    1. Put some copper in the smelters.
    2. Harvest radish and keep them both for cooking. Replant with more radish.
    3. If you were able to plant summer squash on Summer 1 then it should be ready now. Sell at least one, then you can sell the rest or put it in preserves jars to give as loved gifts to Harvey.
    4. Pet chickens.
    5. Put some copper in the smelters.
    6. Pack your bag with: Fishing rod, hoe, and gifts for Wizard, Marnie, Jas, Jodi, Vincent, Emily, Haley, Alex, Willy, and Leah.
    7. Check the traveling cart.
    8. Give a gift to the Wizard.
    9. Give gifts to Marnie and Jas at Marnie's house.
    10. Give gifts to Jodi and Vincent at their house.
    11. Give gifts to Emily and Haley at their house.
    12. Give a gift to Alex at the beach.
    13. Give a gift to Willy at his shop.
    14. Fish at the ocean until 6PM. Around 2PM, check for Leah at the tidepools and give her a gift.
    15. At 6PM go back to your farm and drop off anything you don't need. Pick up gifts for Gus, Shane, Elliot, and Pam. Also pack the following community center donations: grape, spice berry, sweet pea, largemouth bass, carp, bullhead, sturgeon, pufferfish, sandfish, ghostfish, and woodskip. If you were unable to complete the crab pot bundle before, also bring a crab, cockle, oyster, mussel, and clam.
    16. Put some copper in the smelters.
    17. Donate community center items. You can use or sell the dressed spinner, keep the Dish O' The Sea to gift to Linus, and sell the summer seeds.
    18. Go to the Stardrop Saloon to give the gifts.
    19. If you got crab pots from the community center, put them in the ocean and fill them with bait.
    20. Fish at the ocean for the rest of the night.
    21. Put some copper in the smelters.

    7. Pack your bag with: Fishing rod, hoe, and gifts for Evelyn, George, Pierre, Caroline, Abigail, Maru, Harvey, Demetrius, Lewis, Alex, Clint, and Penny. If you have room to bring museum donations or geodes, bring those as well.

    13. Give a gift to Alex at the beach.

    15. Make museum donations if you have any.
    16. Give a gift to Penny on the bridge by the museum, or at her trailer if she isn't on the bridge yet.
    17. Give a gift to Demetrius by the fountain.
    18. If it's raining, fish at the lake until 7PM and then go fishing in the mines. If it's sunny, go fishing at the ocean.
    19. Put copper in the smelters before you go to bed.

    Summer 11, Thursday (Luau)

    1. Put some iron in the smelters.
    2. Keep the two eggs from your chickens for cooking. If you start to run low on space in your cooking chest, make a big chest and replace it.
    3. Chop wood and stone until 12:00pm.
    4. Put some iron in the smelters.
    5. Bring your smoked sturgeon to the beach.
    6. Talk to everyone at the Luau.
    7. Put the smoked sturgeon in the Luau soup and trigger the cut scene.
    8. Do not buy anything from Pierre. He doesn't deserve your money.
    9. Leave the beach and chop wood until bedtime.
    10. Put some iron in the smelters.

    Summer 12, Friday

    1. Put some iron in the smelters.
    2. Don't put any new produce into preserves jars. You will need them to be empty when your starfruit is ready to harvest.
    3. Harvest hops. Keep normal quality to brew and sell silver and gold star quality. You'll harvest this every day for the rest of the summer.
    4. Harvest tomatoes and keep the lowest quality for the Community Center and the rest for cooking.
    5. Harvest peppers and keep them for gifts.
    6. Keep the two eggs from your chickens for cooking.
    7. Check fruit cave.
    8. Put some iron in the smelters.
    9. Pack your bag with: Sword, pickaxe, hoe, 350 wood, 150 stone, and gifts for Wizard, Marnie, Jas, Jodi, Vincent, and Emily, Haley.

    11. Give a gift to the Wizard.

    13. Give gifts to Jodi and Vincent at their house.
    14. Give gifts to Emily and Haley at their house.
    15. Go to Robin's and buy the barn.
    16. Go to the mines until 6PM.
    17. Go back to your farm and drop off anything you don't need. Pick up gifts for Sam, Sebastian, Leah, Clint, Shane, Elliot, and Pam.
    18. Put some iron in the smelters.
    19. Go to the Stardrop Saloon to give the gifts.
    20. Go back to mining.
    21. Put some iron in the smelters.

    Summer 13, Saturday (Alex's Birthday)

    1. Put some gold in the smelters.
    2. Harvest wheat and radishes. Fill all 10 spots with wheat. Keep your two lowest quality radishes for cooking, and sell the two highest quality.
    3. Harvest and replant melons. Keep the melons you need for cooking and donating. Separately, keep all normal quality melons to put in preserves jars when the starfruit are done. Keep one silver or normal quality melon to give to Alex for his birthday, since it's higher quality than the usual parsnips.
    4. Keep the two eggs from your chickens for cooking.
    5. Put some gold in the smelters.
    6. Pack your bag with: Fishing rod, hoe, and gifts for Sandy, Sam, Penny, and Alex.
    7. Give a gift to Alex at the beach.
    8. Give a gift to Penny at the museum.
    9. Go to the desert, collect forage, and buy 16 starfruit seeds. Give a gift to Sandy.
    10. Drop off seeds and desert forage at your farm.
    11. Give a gift to Sam outside his house.
    12. If it's raining, fish at the lake until 7PM and then go fishing in the mines. If it's sunny, pick up forage at the beach and then go fishing at the ocean.
    13. Put some gold in the smelters.

    Summer 14, Sunday

    1. Put some gold in the smelters.
    2. Harvest blueberries and starfruit. Replant starfruit.
    3. Keep 3 lowest quality blueberries, 2 for cooking and 1 for the Community Center. Sell the rest.
    4. Put lowest quality starfruit in your preserves jars. If you don’t fill all the jars, use normal quality melons to fill the remaining ones. Keep a gold star quality starfruit in storage for a quest. Keep all regular quality to put in preserves jars, but sell extra silver and gold quality right away.
    5. If you have the resources, craft 2 mayo machines.
    6. Put the two eggs from your chickens into the mayo machines.
    7. Pack your bag with: Fishing rod, hoe, and gifts for Wizard, Marnie, Jas, Jodi, Vincent, Emily, and Haley.
    8. Check the traveling cart.
    9. Give a gift to the Wizard at his tower.
    10. Give gifts to Marnie and Jas at Marnie's house.
    11. Give gifts to Jodi and Vincent at their house.
    12. Give gifts to Emily and Haley at their house.
    13. Go fishing at Cindersap river until 6pm.
    14. Go back to your farm and drop off anything you don't need. Pick up gifts for Gus, Shane, Elliot, and Pam.
    15. Also bring the following community center donations: tomato, hot pepper, melon, blueberry, red mushroom, cave carrot, morel, coconut, cactus fruit, sardine, red snapper, tuna, tilapia.
    16. Put some gold in the smelters.
    17. Go to the Stardrop Saloon to give the gifts.
    18. Take all donations you have to the community center.
    19. If you still have time before bed, pick up forage at the beach and then gather wood and stone until bedtime.
    20. Put some gold in the smelters.

    6. Bring a gift for Sam and go buy 2 cows and a milk pail from Marnie. I like to get a white cow and a brown cow, but it doesn't actually matter what colours they are.
    7. Give a gift to Sam outside his house.
    8. Go back to your farm to pet the cows and let them out of the barn to eat grass.
    9. Pack your bag with: Sword, pickaxe, hoe, 450 wood, and gifts for Robin and Linus.

    11. Give a gift to Linus by the lake.
    12. Go to the mines for the rest of the day.
    13. Put some gold in the smelters.

    18. Put some gold in the smelters.

    Summer 19, Friday (Demetrius' Birthday)

    1. Put some iron in the smelters.
    2. Craft 8 tappers and put them on the trees you planted earlier. If you can't craft all the tappers now, make as many as you can and try to put at least one on each tree. Prioritize the maple tree first, as you will need maple syrup to make a bee house later. If your trees aren't done growing, make some tree fertilizer for them to speed up the process.
    3. Check the fruit cave.
    4. Pack your bag with: Sword, pickaxe, ax, hoe, fishing rod, food, and gifts for Wizard, Marnie, Jas, Jodi, Vincent, Emily, and Haley.
    5. Check the traveling cart.
    6. Give a gift to the Wizard.
    7. Give gifts to Marnie and Jas at Marnie's house. You can probably grab the purple shorts from Marnie's room now.
    8. Give gifts to Jodi and Vincent at their house.
    9. Give gifts to Emily and Haley at their house.
    10. Collect wood and stone on your farm or around the valley to upgrade the coop and barn until 5 PM. For the next set of upgrades, you'll need 850 wood, 350 stone, and 22,000g.
    11. Go back to your farm and drop off anything you don't need. Pick up gifts for Sam, Sebastian, Leah, Willy, Robin, Demetrius, Gus, Shane, Elliot, and Pam.
    12. Put some iron in the smelters.
    13. Go to the Stardrop Saloon to give the gifts.
    14. Continue to collect wood and stone if you need it, or go to the ocean to fish if you need more money.
    15. Put some iron in the smelters.

    Summer 20, Saturday (Trout Derby)

    5. Put some iron in the smelters.
    6. If you have extra time at the end of the day, clear more wood and stone on your farm.
    7. Put some iron in the smelters.

    Summer 21, Sunday (Trout Derby)

    1. Put some iron in the smelters.
    2. Harvest and replant wheat.
